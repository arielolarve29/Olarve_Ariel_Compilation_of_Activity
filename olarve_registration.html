a. Twenty five percent (25%)of the total fees shall be collected upon enrollment.
b. Remaining seventhy five percent(75%), will be divided into each terms one week before the examination.

b. 50% of the amount paid when he withdraws within the second, third, fourth week after the registration, whether he attended class or not.
c. No refund will be made 30 days after registration.
